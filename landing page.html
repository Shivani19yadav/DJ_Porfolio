I'M KRISHNA

A DJ / Music Consultant based out of Mumbai, India who loves to play open-format sets ranging from Hip Hop, Funk, Soul, Disco, Italo, Electro Swing, House, etc. I've consulted & played music at various Mumbai & Vilnius residencies and played music at venues like Aer Lounge, Taj Hotels , NSCI Dome, and more

Know More






Krishna started his musical journey as a DJ in 2014 while pursuing Computer Engineering in Mumbai, India. Looking at his batch mates performing live arts in the college events motivated him to pursue Djing, which captivated Krishna, since his formative years of schooling.

A self-taught DJ who always strives to put his best foot forward to create a memorable sonic ecosystem for people to have a great time. Be it a bar night, club gigs, or a private bash, Krishna has got the right tunes to set up a lively bubble comprising of a splendid audience, a multi-colored dancefloor, or an intriguing vibe during his lounge sets.

During the six years of his musical career, Krishna has held residencies at The Little Door, Theory - The Experience, Hakkasan in Mumbai, and an International residency at Rumor Lounge & Club in Vilnius, Lithuania. He has played for several soirees organized by Salvation Star - India’s best LGBTQ Community & Brand. He is associated with brands like Le-Mill, Entertainment Bay, to name a few tailoring the DJ sets matching the brands vision & style.

Being exposed to various genres & cultures, Krishna knows the right way to create a very niche & international-sounding experience be it Mexican, Latin, French, or Spanish themed parties, He is apt to make any dance floor/gig- going crowd pogo to their heart's content. He is current focus lies in increasing his musical repertoire by creating DJ sets comprising of Vinyl Only Selections of Warmer Sounds from Across the Globe.

People express the feeling of utter joy, the warm smiles when they are having a great time at a bar, club. The audience forgets about their worries & loses out for the moment in amalgamation with the finely calibrated music. That vibe is contagious & it is the sole reason why Krishna likes DJing & stays in a feedback loop to keep the experience healthy & fun for his audience.
